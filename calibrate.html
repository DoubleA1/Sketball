🏀 Shot Detector Calibration

How to run a session: Start the camera, hit Record, then do 15–20 real shooting motions, tapping (or holding) SPACEBAR right as you finish each shot — or have someone press it for you so you can shoot two-handed. On phones, hold the orange button instead. Between shots, also spend ~20 seconds doing things that should NOT count: talk, nod, lean, walk across frame — without pressing space. Then Stop & Analyze, copy the data, and paste it back to Claude.
